Welcome to First Stride Coaching

Your first step towards achieving your running goals! Whether you’re taking your initial steps into running, aiming to smash a personal best, or training for marathons and beyond, we’re here to guide, support, and push you to new heights. Here’s why runners of all levels choose us:


  • Personalized Training Plans: Tailored to your goals, fitness level, your running environment, and lifestyle.

  • Expert Coaching: Benefit from certified coaches who are experienced runners themselves.

  • Flexible Coaching Options: Choose a plan that fits your preferences and budget.

  • Welcoming to All: We welcome runners of all backgrounds and experiences, especially if you’ve never worked with a coach before.

How Does it Work?

Complete a questionnaire to help our coaches understand your needs and lifestyle, allowing them to create a customized running plan to achieve your goals.

Work alongside our coaches to tweak your plan before and during your training.
